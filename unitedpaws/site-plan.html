Content

Home page

This page will contain an image slider with 3 images of lost pets along a welcome message and our slogan. Welcome Message "Welcome to UnitedPaws, help your 4 paws buddies to find their way back home"

Below image slider there will be displayed instructions on how to aboard/approach a lost pet and most recent statistic facts related to this matter

Text to present over image slider: At least 245 pets are lost every week in Uruguay and it is important to mention that approach to them must be cautious for preventing them to run away even further.

Seasonal warnings related to weather that may affect critically any lost pet along an image related.
