Frequently Asked Questions

Payment methods

Currently we accept Mastercard, American Express card, Discover card, PayPal, Apple Pay and Google Pay.

What is included on the price?

Be aware that Whitewater Rafting Co. offer several packages which include a variety of activities, therefore this will set the price to pay out.

Is operative all the year?

At Whitewater Rafting Co. we work the whole year except holidays, check the list.

Operative Hours

From Monday to Friday > 8:00 - 15:00 and Saturday > 10:00 - 17:00

Is there any assistance for emergencies?

Our guides are certified lifeguards and first aid technicians.

Is this a good trip for kids?

We encourage all parents/guardians to allow their children to live this experience as it mixes nature, discipline and fun.

What are the age ranges you allow?

Absolutely this is a good trip for children of all ages. We have found however that children over the age of 8 seem to enjoy it more overall.

Is whitewater rafting safe?

As with all adventure sports, there is an inherent and assumed element of risk. To help ensure your safety everyone on commercial rafting trips wears coast guard approved life vest or personal flotation device (PFD)

What do I need to bring?

We suggest bringing sunscreen, a hat and a change of clothes.

What will the children learn from the experience?

Children will learn discipline, commitment to others, teamwork, respect, decision-making, among others.

Are there other activities?

Yes, it is important for us to offer a unique experience to all our visitors. We offer additionally hiking, handcraft, archery, among others.

Is there any accommodation?

We are working to improve our facilities to acommodate better our visitors, soon we'll have cabins with private bathroom. Currently we offer tiki tents and community bathroom.

Are there any restaurant?

Yes, we have different menus (vegan, vegetarian, diabetic, celiac, etc).

Is there any reward program?

We encourage children to engage with the experience so they will earn scores during each activity. At the end of each day, the TOP 10 will receive points to redeem in our gift shop.

Is there a gift shop?

In our gift shop you'll be able to buy a lots of things. Among them we highlight printed photo of amazing experience in our activities, custom pods, souvenirs.
